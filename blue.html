Blue

As a cool color, blue has a very calming and tranquil effect. It is often associated with feelings of trust and productivity. It is also said
to give people a feeling of peace and serenity, which is why the color is often utilized by healthcare websites. Instead of giving off high energy
and creating a sense of excitement like the color red does, blue has the opposite effect, so it is usually used for a more peaceful setting. The
different variations of the color blue also have a large impact. Compared to the lighter shades of blue, the darker shades can stimulate the mind,
but can also create feelings of depression and coldness.
