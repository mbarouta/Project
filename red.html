Red

Being a bright and warm color, red typically creates a sense of urgency and excitement for a web page viewer. Red is used as an attention grabber, ergo it is known as a call-to-action color.
Red is also known to cause faster breathing and increase the heart rate, it gives off intense and passionate feelings, so it would make more sense to use the color in a high energy or exciting setting
rather than a calm or low energy setting. A darker shade of red can have a different meaning. For example, a dark shade of red could give off an authoritative, luxurious aesthetic.
